# LBC Express, Inc. v. Ado

Summary: Euberto Ado, an OFW, entrusted his passport with a re-entry visa to LBC for customs processing of his balikbayan boxes. LBC lost the passport, stopping Euberto from returning to his job in Bahrain. He sued for lost income, but the Supreme Court ruled he didn't prove the exact amount.
